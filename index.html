The Demonetized Web!

"Google sucks now"

But it's not just Google. The problem is that sites have taken on an extractive revenue model because it allows them to survive and thrive in the current landscape.

The internet's highest value is its ability to curate and filter community knowledge, but it has become so bloated, filled with scripts, trackers, and ads that it is absolutely miserable to use. The best or most relevant results to your search query are obscured by thousands of sites that have SEO'd (search engine optimized) to steal your attention and bait you into watching ads.

Here is a great 3rd party demonstration of the problem.

Our core philosophy is that the highest value of a community will arise when members produce content purely to share, rather than to profit. Hence the demonetized. Our ultimate goal is to spread awareness of the problem, and compile a list of resources to provide people with a better way.

This site is very much so a work in progress. It will likely stay that way until the end of time. So far Vladimir Prelovac has taken this idea much farther, beyond my technical ability. He is responsible for both kagi.com and teclis.com, which are two amazing solutions I have found so far for the demonetized web.
